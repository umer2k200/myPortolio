Ballebaaz – Cricket App

A comprehensive cricket management app for local matches.

Built in React Native and Firebase, Ballebaaz supports live scoring, match booking, AI-powered DRS (LBW detection), highlights generation, stats tracking, and a custom AI kit generator. Roles include players, team owners, umpires, and ground owners.
